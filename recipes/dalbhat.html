Dalbhat

Description

This recipe is for a lovely nepali staple dish using lentil soup and white rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 3 cup water
  • 2 tablespoon mixed lentil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp oil
Steps
  1. Wash rice 3 times with cold water and add 1 cup water and cook in ricecooker.
  2. Heat a pressure cooker with oil in medium heat.
  3. Wash lentil mix 3 times and add it to the heated oil pan.
  4. Fry lentil for 2 min and add turmeric while stirring.
  5. Add 2 cup water to the lentil and salt while stirring.
  6. Cover pressur cooker and cook under medium heat until 4 steam releases occur.
