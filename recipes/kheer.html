Kheer

Description

This recipe is for a lovely rice pudding dish

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 3 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • 1/2 cup split cashews
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut and raisin mix
Steps
  1. Wash rice 3 times with cold water and add milk and cook in ricecooker over slow heat.
  2. Once milk thickens add sugar cardamom, cashwes, coconut and rasins.
  3. Keep stirring for 25-30 min over low heat until the rice is cooked.
  4. Let cool before serving. For summer time refrigerate and serve cold
