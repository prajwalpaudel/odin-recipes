Omlette

Description

This recipe is for a lovely Omlette breakfast

Ingredients

  • 2 brown eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ginger powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
Steps
  1. Heat non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Crack 2 eggs over a bowl and mix.
  3. Add milk, ginger, onion, tomatoes and mix evenly.
  4. Pour into the hot pan and cook both sides evenly. Sprinkle salt before serving.
